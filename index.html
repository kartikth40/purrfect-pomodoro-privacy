Purrfect Pomodoro Timer

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Purrfect Pomodoro Timer is designed to help you manage your time effectively and stay focused. Your privacy is important to us. This extension is built with privacy in mind.

🔒 What we don’t collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any direct personal information.
  • No browsing history, website content, or personal activity is transmitted to any server.

🌐 Permissions

Purrfect Pomodoro Timer requests certain permissions that may look broad (such as the ability to “read and change data on all websites”). We want to be clear:

✅ These permissions are only used to enable features you control — specifically:

  • To block or redirect websites that you choose during focus sessions (using declarativeNetRequest).
  • To ensure your productivity sessions are distraction-free as you’ve configured them.
We do not access or modify the content of websites you visit, beyond enforcing the site blocking you’ve set up.

📊 Analytics

Purrfect Pomodoro Timer uses PostHog, a privacy-conscious analytics platform, to help us understand how the extension is used so we can improve it over time.

We collect non-personally identifiable information, such as:

  • Anonymous user IDs (generated by PostHog or our extension)
  • Events related to timers, focus sessions, and site blocking (including the domains/hostnames you choose to block)
  • Feature usage (e.g., enabling settings, starting/stopping timers)

This data is used solely to improve the extension’s functionality and user experience. We do not sell or share this data with other third parties.

We believe in transparency and privacy-first design. Our permissions and data collection are limited to what’s necessary to deliver the features you choose to use.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at kartik.pomodoro@gmail.com.
