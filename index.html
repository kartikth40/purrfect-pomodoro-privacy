🐾 Purrfect Pomodoro Timer – Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Purrfect Pomodoro Timer is designed to help you manage your time effectively and stay focused. Your privacy is important to us. This extension is built with privacy in mind:

  • ✅ We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
  • ✅ All site blocking settings are stored locally in your browser.
  • ✅ No browsing history, website content, or personal activity is transmitted to any server.
  • ✅ We request host permissions and use declarativeNetRequest solely to block websites you specify during focus sessions.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at your.email@example.com.
